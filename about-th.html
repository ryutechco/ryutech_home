Who we are

We are passionate engineers in Electronics and Automotives. Our vision is to be the front line of Thailand in developing embedded electronics in automotive industry. We values passion, knowledge, will, learnability, and trust.

Ryutech Co., Ltd. was established in 2005 first to start trading CNC milling and griding machines. In the late 2017 the direction chages to Embedded Systems and Automotive. We start providing OEM ECU remapping services in partnetship with the UK's leading remapping company.
