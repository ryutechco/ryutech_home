Welcome
to Ryutech
Automotive
Tuning Center

We are the specialist in automotive engine tuning and electrical systems. At Ryutech Automotive Tuning Center, we are fully equipped with an industrial grade dynamometer test cell training/workshop facilities, and range of equipments for tuning.

We provide engine tuning solutions, products, and training matching our client’s needs. We have partnered with Dimsport, Dyno Developments, and Quantumtuning to give our clients a one stop with full range of services and products.

How engine tuning works ?

The power output of the engine comes from combustion of air/fuel mixture. When we make alterations that affect the combustion, the power output of the engine changes. This can be done by altering the controls of Fuel Injection and/or Ignition from the Engine Control Unit (ECU).

What is a
dynometer ?

Dynamometer is an equipment that measures power output of the car’s engine at the wheels without having to drive on the road or race track. The power output are usually measured in the unit of Torque, and Horsepower. There are many types of Dynamometer, the most common type is a rolling road dynamometer.
